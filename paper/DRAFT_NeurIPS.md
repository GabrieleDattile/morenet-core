# Spectral Morphogenetic Resonance Networks (MoReNet): A Backpropagation-Free Topology for Continuous Fluidic Learning

## Abstract
The current paradigm of Artificial Intelligence, dominated by connectionist architectures and the backpropagation algorithm, faces insurmountable limits related to global energy consumption and catastrophic forgetting. In this paper, we present a radically new computational approach inspired by fluid dynamics, cellular morphogenesis, and spectral graph theory. 

We introduce Spectral Morphogenetic Resonance Networks (MoReNet), an architecture devoid of arbitrary multiplicative weights and gradients, in which information propagates as a differential wave equation along the Laplacian of a high-dimensional non-Euclidean graph. Learning emerges naturally not as iterative optimization, but as Spectral Topological Erosion.
